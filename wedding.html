Wedding Planning

The wedding can be considered as an act of epithalamium. Wedding Planning involves endless details, deadlines so as to make the wedding elope. The planning procedure starts with the question of deciding the place suitable and decent for the wedding. So in order to simplify the search, the wedding planners at events of happiness offer perfect place with detailing of the wedding halls, and dream destination wedding venues, suitable for the wedding.

Wedding planners at events of happiness also provide the greatest and the most attractive designs for the decoration of the place too. An example to demonstrate this that couple can arrives in a classic vintage car, or a sleek limousine.Then comes the decoration of the stage for the different wedding stages.

Wedding stage decoration may contain numerous elements such as traditional flowers, oriental flowers, curtains, drapes, crystals, decorative stones, and other soft decorative elements. Each wedding stage is customized and their tastes and opinions are depicted accordingly in the design with due care given to lightening, movement and flow of the designing team 3-D models of the stage to display how the wedding stage would look on wedding day exactly.

After finalization of wedding dates and venues the next thing these planners help in is with the type of the invitation cards by having subtle words and design depending upon the client preference and according to the latest trends including eco-friendly cards.

Velvet Venue is such a company who believes in serving their clients to the fullest which is very well depicted in their work. According to the theme we arrange for the caterers and provide the best espousal and confetti. We also help in selecting morganatic and all the other types of gifts to be exchanged between the two parties. Henceforth we definitely believe in establishing your vision and assembling a group of trusted co-planners and sticking as best you can to clear roles and expectations will set you up for a happier wedding.
